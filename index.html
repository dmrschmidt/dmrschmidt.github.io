— A short introduction

Hi, I'm Dennis.

Berlin, DE
he/him
DS · 2026

I make things that spark joy — apps and open-source libraries, cocktail bitters and techno tracks, photographs and the occasional fridge magnet.

For two decades I've worked at the intersection of software, sound and image. Shipping iOS apps for millions of people. Releasing records under the moniker MTZTGR. Quietly building a body of photographs alongside it all.

I lead software engineering by day. By evening I'm in the studio, the kitchen, or somewhere with a camera. The work is varied; the throughline is craft.
